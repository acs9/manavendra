Those were the Days

Those were the Days is Manavendra's debut book. It is a collection of short stories about young boys growing up in a boarding school – an easy to read commercial fiction.

Read on 
An era before Google...

You are never more alive than as a teenager. With numerous bodily changes and with the brain flush with chemicals, teenage is a time of great tumult, especially for young men in an all-boys’ boarding school. 

More

Jai and his friends provide enough opportunities to hark back to those days of being younger. They remind us of when we acted saucy with our teacher or for that matter benefitted from a father figure in form of another teacher. They take us down the memory to feel how it was to have a crush on the girl in the school across the road or fancied a teacher in school.

More ... 

In their stories is the exhilaration of bunking school, the grit of fighting it out on the playing field and of course the heady feeling of falling in love. The pressure of examinations, the thrill of adventure and matters of honour are integral to their stories.
